Introduction

Spiritfarer is an indie adventure game developed by Thunder Lotus Games, where the player must meet the needs of the spirits aboard their ship with foods and building boat additions.

This leads to the player collecting materials while on their journey to build kitchens, gardens, and other home amenities to help the spirits feel more at home.

While uncovering more of each spirit's backstory, they sail their way to crossing over to the afterlife through the Everdoor.

These new sections that are added to the ship are placed like puzzle pieces, stacked up on top of one another in any way that fits.

The characters that Stella meets along the way also give the player mini-games to play for materials and in-game currency that are relevant to the spirit's character.

The game also has vendors that sell items such as seeds for the player's gardens, and furniture to decorate their ship, giving the game a more personalized experience.
